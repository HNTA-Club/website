The year comes to and end and with it the club's board of 2018 as well.

As such the positions for the next year's board are opening and so we invite you to:

**HNTA's board groping**

**Held on Tuesday 06.11.2018 starting at 18:00 at the clubroom**

With this event we are giving you a chance familiarize yourselves with how the club's board works and what the people on the board actually do.
During the event you can preapply for the next year's board(Fuksis are given fuksipoints for joining a club's/guild's board). (The actual board joining process is done during the fall general meeting, that is soon to follow.)

So gather up your courage and join the event to meet the current board members and perchance some of the new ones as well!

If the prestigious award of a position in the next year's board is not enough to quench your thirst for power and glory then at least come and enjoy some light refreshments as we tell you tales of the board's grandeur.

In the occasion you are unable to participate in the event, but would be interested in joining the board, or have any questions send an email to the board's mailing list "hnta-hallitus@listmail.tut.fi" or poke one of the current board members on irc (@ircnet #hnta)/ discord (https://discord.gg/uZJqHH7)

PS: The doors to the basement where our clubroom is located are locked at 18, so if you're stuck outside ping us in irc or discord and we'll come open the doors for you.
