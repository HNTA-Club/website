HeNTAi is TUT's anime club, which organizes animation and manga related activities for the students of TUT.

Students can join the club for a tiny fee of 0 ¥.

The clubroom is situated in the basement of Sähkötalo.

In the clubroom you can read manga, enjoy audiovisual entertainment or just have a breather when other guilds and clubs are busy.

At the club you can buy embroidered patches, snacks and drinks.

The clubroom is usually open on weekdays between 12-18 depending on the schedules of the board.

To see if the clubroom is open or not you can check the club's Slack or IRC and look for Aniki-chan's announcements or "That pink door" on Slack.
