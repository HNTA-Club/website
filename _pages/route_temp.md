# Route to temporary clubroom

<ol>
    <li>Enter Festia building lobby (Festia A) in Herwanta. There is no keycard reader on the Festia door so go through the Main building if the door is locked.</li>
    <li>Go to Festia A floor 3 using the stairs or the elevator<li>
    <li>Walk the corridor to Festia B (the door will be locked)</li>
    <li>Check the <code>#events</code> channel on Discord and ping a board member present at the clubroom to ask them to open the door.</li>
</ol>
